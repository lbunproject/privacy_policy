Privacy Policy WESO Cloud Miner

How we collect, use, and share information — and the choices you have.

Last updated: August 20, 2025

This Privacy Policy governs how WESO Cloud Miner (the “App”) collects, uses, and shares information. It also explains the choices you have. By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

Summary (What’s new)

  • We have two online features (leaderboard & cloud miner).
  • These features send limited, consent-based data to our server: user name or ID, (for leaderboard) and aggregate screen taps (used to compute mining power).
  • You can decline or revoke consent at any time in Settings → Data & Privacy; if you do, online features are disabled.

Data We Collect

1) Data you provide (only if you consent)

  • Name/ID (for the public leaderboard). You can use any nickname — do not use your real name.

2) Gameplay activity we generate (only if you consent)

  • Token pops / tap activity (aggregated counts used to derive your mining power).
  • Leaderboard stats derived from gameplay (e.g., rounds won and rewards).

3) Technical data collected automatically when online features are used

When the App connects to our server (e.g., via WebSocket), standard server logs may include: IP address, timestamps, App version, and error/diagnostic events. This is typical for operating, securing, and troubleshooting online services. We do not collect precise device identifiers for advertising.

If you do not consent, the App does not connect to our servers for gameplay data, and the items above are not transmitted.

How We Use Your Data

  • Enable online gameplay features: public leaderboard and cloud miner matching your mining power.
  • Attribute rewards to your name/ID.
  • Operate, secure, and improve the service (e.g., detect abuse, debug issues, ensure availability).

We DO NOT sell your data. We DO NOT use it for targeted advertising.

Legal Basis

Consent. We process leaderboard and cloud-miner data only if you grant consent on first launch (or later in Settings). You may withdraw consent at any time; online features will be disabled immediately.

Your Choices & Controls

  • Grant/withdraw consent: Settings → Data & Privacy.
  • Change display name: Settings → Data & Privacy.
  • Data access/deletion: Email lbunproject@gmail.com with Name/ID so we can locate your records.

Data Sharing

We share data only as needed to operate the service:

  • Service providers / infrastructure (e.g., hosting, DDoS protection, logging). These processors act on our instructions and use safeguards to protect your data.
  • Legal compliance & safety if required by applicable law or to protect our rights, users, or the public.

We DO NOT share data with advertisers.

Data Retention

  • Leaderboard / rewards records: kept as long as necessary to provide the feature or until you request deletion.
  • Server logs: retained for a limited period for security and operational integrity, then deleted or anonymized.

If you revoke consent, we stop further collection going forward.

Security

We use technical and organizational measures (including transport encryption) to protect data in transit and at rest. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we strive to use industry-accepted safeguards.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us so we can delete it.

Third-Party Platform Policies

If you install the App via a platform (e.g., Apple App Store), that platform may collect aggregated, anonymous crash or performance data under its own privacy policy. Such platform-level collection is separate from our processing.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post updates in the App or on our website. Changes take effect when posted.
